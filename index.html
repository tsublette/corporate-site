About

Our journey from idea to product

  • 2009-2013

    Research Beginnings

    Intensive period of academic research, tackling the problems related to simulating a full scale additive manufacturing process.

  • 2014

    3DSIM is Born

    Critical mass of research success resulted in the formation of 3DSIM. First funding round completed, initial team assembled, and work building the world’s fastest simulation framework began.

  • January 2015

    Accelerated Growth

    Relocated the company to Park City, UT to access the large Salt Lake City technical talent pool.

  • 2015-2016

    Building Our Vision

    Grew the team by 4x. Completed structural mechanics and thermal solvers. Built cloud delivery framework. Engaged alpha users.

  • September 2016

    exaSIM Beta Launch!

    The first easy to use cloud-based simulation tool for predicting stress and distortion in metal laser sintering.

Predicting stress and distortion trends
for
intelligent support generation

It is estimated that tens of thousands of dollars are wasted per machine per year in unnecessary support material costs, post manufacturing labor part finishing, and material lost due to failed builds. Our simulation tools predict residual stress and strain data to identify the minimum amount of support needed for your part.
